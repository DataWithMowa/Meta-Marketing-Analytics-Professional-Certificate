# Course 1: Marketing Analytics Foundation

This folder contains my notes, projects, practice assignments and coursework for **Course 1: Marketing Analytics Foundation**, which is part of the Meta Marketing Analytics Professional Certificate.

## 📖 What I Learnt

* **Data-Driven Insights:** How data and its structural measurement inform real-world marketing actions and business decisions.
* **Core Fundamentals:** The basic principles of marketing, including audience segmentation, business metrics, and tracking the customer journey.
* **Data Governance:** How marketing data is collected, managed, and integrated, along with a deep dive into the legal regulations and compliance laws around information privacy.
